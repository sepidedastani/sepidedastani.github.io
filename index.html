FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a gym membership?

Not required. Programs can be designed for home or gym. In-person sessions happen at partner facilities.

What equipment do I need at home?

A pair of adjustable dumbbells and resistance bands are enough to begin. We’ll scale up as needed.

What’s your cancellation policy?

24-hour notice for in-person sessions. Online coaching is billed monthly and can be cancelled any cycle.
